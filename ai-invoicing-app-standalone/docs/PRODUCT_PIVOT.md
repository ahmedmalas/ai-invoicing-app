# Product Pivot: AI Business OS

## Summary
The product is no longer scoped as an invoicing-only app. It is now defined as an AI Business OS for personal users, businesses, accountants/bookkeepers/BAS agents, and future enterprise users.

## Why This Pivot
- Users need a single operating layer for documents, payment workflows, tracking, and financial administration.
- Invoicing is critical but insufficient as the whole product model.
- Shared AI memory, profile data, and timeline history become more valuable when all related workflows live in one platform.

## Core Product Commitments
1. Support both personal and business use.
2. Include tiering for Personal, Business, Professional, and future Enterprise.
3. Store documents securely and support search/retrieval.
4. Convert uploaded invoices/finals/documents into user-preferred branded style.
5. Let users upload logos and support guided branding direction.
6. Recommend layouts, colors, styles, wording, fields, and structure based on preferences.
7. Remember user preferences and previous decisions over time.
8. Support reusable templates and repeat workflows.
9. Treat invoices as the first document type only.
10. Design as document platform first and AI Business OS overall.
11. Include advisor collaboration with secure permission-based access.
12. Keep standalone operation primary; integrations remain optional extensions.

## Implications for Delivery
- Slice 1 remains invoice draft-to-PDF, but must align to AI Business OS entities and timeline.
- Data model must support customer/supplier/advisor profiles and memory scaffolding from the start.
- Payment and reminder flows should include uncertainty-safe states and auditable actions.
- Advanced bank-feed automation and deep integrations can be deferred behind stable manual workflows.
