# AI Business OS Blueprint

## Vision Statement
Ai Invoicing App evolves into an AI Business OS that unifies document workflows, payment workflows, profile intelligence, advisor collaboration, and operational visibility for personal users and businesses.

## User Segments
- Personal users.
- Businesses.
- Professional advisors (accountants, bookkeepers, BAS agents).
- Enterprise teams (future).

## Platform Pillars
1. Document intelligence and conversion.
2. Persistent AI memory and recommendation quality.
3. Universal search as a core platform service.
4. Profile-driven and job-driven operations (customer, supplier, advisor, job).
5. Payment and reminder workflows with auditability.
6. Timeline-first observability and compliance-ready records.
7. User ownership portability (export, backup, migration, import).
8. Professional-quality generated outputs.
9. Standalone-first architecture with optional integrations.

## Document Coverage
- Documents, invoices, final invoices, quotes, receipts, bills, statements, timesheets, delivery dockets, purchase orders, contracts, letters, forms, custom documents.

## AI Memory Principle
If a user has to do something twice, the product should explain why it did not remember and adapt.

## Edition Model
- Personal Edition
- Business Edition
- Professional Edition
- Enterprise (future)

## Delivery Principle
Invoice lifecycle is the first vertical slice. Every design decision should preserve expansion to the full AI Business OS scope.

## UX Principle
Simple first, powerful second. New users should not feel overwhelmed, and advanced capability should unlock naturally.
