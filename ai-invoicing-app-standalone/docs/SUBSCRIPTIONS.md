# Subscription Strategy

## Product Plans
- Personal
- Business
- Professional
- Enterprise (future)

## Plan Intent
- Personal: individual and simple document workflows.
- Business: broader operations, teams, and business workflows.
- Professional: advisor-centric workflows for accountants/bookkeepers/BAS agents.
- Enterprise: advanced controls, governance, and scale (future).

## Pricing Communication Guardrail
Do not promise universal tax deductibility.

Approved language baseline:
- Business software subscriptions may be deductible depending on user circumstances and local tax rules.
- Users should confirm deductibility and treatment with their accountant or tax advisor.
