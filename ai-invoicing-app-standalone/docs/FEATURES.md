# Features (Foundation Draft)

## Core Functional Areas

1. Invoice Authoring
   - Create, edit, duplicate, and preview invoices.
   - Dynamic line item management (quantity, rate, discounts, tax inclusion).

2. Layout and Template Customization
   - Fully configurable invoice sections and field visibility.
   - Reusable templates with saved branding and formatting rules.

3. Customer and Billing Profiles
   - Customer records with billing and contact metadata.
   - Default payment terms and tax preferences per customer.

4. GST and Tax Handling
   - GST-aware totals and per-line tax configuration.
   - Clear tax summaries for compliance-friendly invoices.

5. Invoice Numbering and Metadata
   - Configurable numbering schemes and prefixes.
   - Issue dates, due dates, references, and status tracking.

6. Payment Details
   - Configurable bank/account and payment instruction blocks.
   - Optional payment notes and remittance references.

7. Export and Sharing
   - PDF export for delivery and archival.
   - Print-ready output fidelity.

8. AI Assistance
   - Draft generation for invoice descriptions and line item suggestions.
   - AI-assisted template and wording optimization.

## Non-Goals (Current Step)
- Final implementation details.
- UI/UX screens and interactions.
- Backend/service architecture.
