# Jobs Model

## Principle
Jobs are first-class platform entities. Everything related to work performed should be linkable to a Job.

## Job Relationship Scope
A Job may contain or link:
- Customer
- Supplier(s)
- Quotes
- Invoices
- Purchase Orders
- Delivery Dockets
- Photos
- Videos
- Files
- Expenses
- Notes
- Timeline
- Payments
- AI Memory

## Model Intent
- Make job context the connective tissue for operational history.
- Reduce fragmented records by encouraging linked entities.
- Improve search and retrieval through job-based grouping.
