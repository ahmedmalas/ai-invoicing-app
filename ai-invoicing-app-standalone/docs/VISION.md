# Vision

## Product Name
Ai Invoicing App

## Mission
Enable personal users, businesses, and professional advisors to run document and money workflows from one AI Business OS while preserving full manual control and auditability.

## Core Concept
This product is a personal + business document platform and operating layer for business administration. Users can upload or create documents, the platform understands and extracts key details, stores them securely, and recreates or converts them into preferred branded formats.

## Product Positioning
- Document platform first.
- Invoicing app second.
- Invoices are the first delivery slice, not the product boundary.
- AI memory is a core behavior, not an optional add-on.

## Core Goal
Deliver a standalone AI Business OS with effectively unlimited customization options for document structure, branding, delivery, tracking, and workflow automation.

## Outcome Targets
- Support personal + business workflows for documents and financial operations.
- Support document types including invoices, final invoices, quotes, receipts, bills, statements, timesheets, delivery dockets, purchase orders, contracts, letters, forms, and custom documents.
- Provide secure document vault storage with search and retrieval.
- Enable upload, extraction, recreation, conversion, branding, sending, tracking, and lifecycle timelines.
- Offer Personal, Business, Professional, and future Enterprise editions.
- Persist AI memory for preferences, branding decisions, layout choices, writing style, payment terms, recurring patterns, and user decisions.
- Offer logo upload plus guided branding direction for users without a logo.
- Provide accountant/bookkeeper/BAS-agent collaboration through permission-based access.
- Deliver universal search as a mandatory core service spanning all major entities and activity history.
- Treat Jobs as first-class operational containers linking work, documents, media, payments, notes, and timeline.
- Ensure every meaningful action is recorded in an immutable timeline for auditability.
- Guarantee user data ownership with export, backup, migration, and import pathways.
- Enforce professional-quality generated documents that are immediately send-ready.
- Follow progressive complexity: simple workflows first, advanced capability growth without overwhelm.
