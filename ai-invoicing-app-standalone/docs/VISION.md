# Vision

## Product Name
Ai Invoicing App

## Mission
Enable businesses to generate and manage invoices faster with AI assistance while preserving full manual control.

## Core Goal
Deliver a standalone AI-assisted invoicing app with effectively unlimited customization options for invoice structure and presentation.

## Outcome Targets
- Flexible invoice creation for varied business models.
- Reusable templates for repeatable workflows.
- Reliable GST handling and payment detail configuration.
- Professional PDF outputs suitable for client delivery and records.
- Extensible architecture for future advanced automation.
