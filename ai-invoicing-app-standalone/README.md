# Ai Invoicing App (Standalone)

This repository contains the documentation-first foundation for **Ai Invoicing App**, now positioned as an **AI Business OS**.

## Product Positioning

- Document platform first.
- Invoicing app second.
- Designed for personal users, businesses, accountants/bookkeepers/BAS agents, and future enterprise users.

## Core Product Goal

Build a standalone AI-assisted platform that supports document creation, upload, storage, search, recreation, conversion, branding, sending, tracking, and lifecycle management across personal and business workflows.

## Architecture Freeze Foundations

The following capabilities are mandatory and architecture-defining:

- Universal Search as a core platform service across documents, customers, suppliers, photos, logos, receipts, invoices, quotes, purchase orders, delivery dockets, contracts, payments, expenses, notes, AI memories, jobs, attachments, and timeline events.
- Jobs as a first-class entity that links work artifacts end-to-end.
- Immutable platform-wide timeline/audit events for meaningful actions.
- User ownership guarantees: easy export, full backup, migration/import, and no vendor lock-in.
- Professional quality standards for generated documents.
- Progressive complexity UX: simple first, advanced power naturally unlocks.

Initial and target document coverage:

- Invoices and final invoices
- Quotes
- Receipts
- Bills
- Statements
- Timesheets
- Delivery dockets
- Purchase orders
- Contracts
- Letters
- Forms
- Custom documents

## Product Editions

- Personal Edition
- Business Edition
- Professional Edition (tax accountants/bookkeepers/BAS agents)
- Enterprise (future tier)
